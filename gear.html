GEAR NAME YEAR COOLEST FEATURES
*Alesis Nitro kit 2022 It's easy to record with and has MIDI!
*Behringer RD-8 mk II 2023 After getting to record with a TR-808 I knew I needed one of these :D
*Behringer RD-9 2024 Huge 909 sound without the huge 909 price
*Behringer TD-3 2021 The 303 sound is legendary, and this rises to the challenge!
*Boss RV-5 2025 A useful reverb pedal, but this one was used in the recording of 'Anna Sun' and other songs from Walk the Moon!!
*Casio CZ-1000 2022 It has amazing low end and can make really cool unique sounds
*Korg microKORG 2023 I sold my original one years ago and wanted another!
*Motu M2 2025 It's got a super-high sample rate and is dead simple to use
*Nord Stage 3 2020 It's incredibly easy to dial in fantastic sounds and it feels amazing to play
*Roland JV-1080 2024 TONS of 90s/2000s sounds, a nice creamy filter, and awesome expansion boards!
*Squier CV 50's Stratocaster 2022 It has a gorgeous aged look and it plays and sounds great
*Squier CV 70's Jazz Bass 2022 I love the playability of the maple neck, and it's got the iconic j-bass sound
*Yamaha PAC-112J 2008 This was my first guitar :D
