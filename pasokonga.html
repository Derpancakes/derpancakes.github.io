This not-so-secret page is dedicated to Tatsuo Horiuchi, an artist whose medium is the venerable Mcirosoft Excel! It appears that his website became defunct in late 2024, so it's likely that a lot of his art has become lost media. I was able to find two of his works in the original Excel format, and wanted to make them available to others. All rights belong to Horiuchi-san, and I claim no ownership.
